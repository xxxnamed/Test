## Why ViChord?

ViChord is a powerful, open-source Android music player focused on **seamless integration with YouTube and YouTube Music**. Get complete control over your listening experience with high-quality playback, background caching, and built-in lyrics synchronization‚Äîall without intrusive ads or dependence on Google Play Services.

---

## Features

### Core Music Experience
* **Play (almost)** any audio from multiple sources ‚Äî mainly YouTube
* **Keep listening** in the background or even offline ‚Äî with cached songs
* **Search through** songs, albums, artists, videos, and playlists

### Integration and Convenience
* **Import your** playlists straight from YouTube
* **Open any** YouTube or YouTube Music link (videos, playlists, channels, etc.) right in ViChord
* **Android Auto** ready

### Quality and Customization
* **View lyrics** ‚Äî fetch, edit, and even sync them
* **Cloud-sync your** playlists seamlessly
* **Enjoy an** optimized listening experience with audio normalization
